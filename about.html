It all started with an Idea.

Education is the most powerful catalyst for social change. A child’s early years are the foundation for accessing a better future. Our holistic approach to child-welfare addresses the needs of children at Anganwadis, Government Schools, Child Care Institutions, rural areas and underserved urban settlements. Identifying the need to comprehensively tackle locally contextualized social issues, our programs adopt a holistic approach to develop a conducive ecosystem for underprivileged children.

Motive

At Early Childhood Education Centers in rural India, our cloud-based technology platform is utilising the strength of cutting-edge data engineering to catalyse pedagogical innovation. We are giving Anganwadi teachers access to interactive academic content in vernacular languages that has been specially vetted. Our digital education is enhancing value-based education, core reading, and numeracy.

Smart Anganwadi

We offer a 40-module, specially created 4-language curriculum through the Smart Anganwadi Initiative, as well as teacher training sessions and regular child assessments.In accordance with the Chili Pili curriculum, which has been approved by the Department of Women and Child Development, we have set up an interactive touch LED panel with customised digital material. Our curriculum strives to establish the groundwork for future schooling and learning by utilising the brain's rapid neurological development during early childhood. ​.
